What is the Impact Ladder? (Beginner On-Ramp)

Tool: the app (Flip, Sheets, an LLM).

Use: how you’ll use it this week (be specific).

Behavior: the routine people will do (e.g. peer feedback every Wed).

Outcome: a measurable change in 2–6 weeks (+1 rubric level, −30 % errors).

Evidence: what you’ll collect to prove it (samples, counts, screenshots).

Tip: Write the behavior as a calendar habit — if it’s not scheduled, it won’t stick.
